# JTDOS FAQ 100

## Real Business Rules FAQ Seed

These FAQ answers follow `docs/REAL_BUSINESS_RULES_V1.md` and override earlier placeholder answers.

## 1. Is the AI quote a confirmed booking?

No. The AI quote is an estimated booking request, not final confirmation.

Final price, vehicle availability, and driver arrangement require operator confirmation by Liu Xiang.

## 2. When is the booking finally confirmed?

The booking is finally confirmed only after operator Liu Xiang confirms the final price, vehicle availability, driver arrangement, and deposit.

## 3. Is a deposit required?

Yes. If the customer accepts the quote, a 20% deposit of the total order amount is required through the operator's PayPal to secure the booking.

## 4. What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation is free if the customer cancels 7 days or more before the service date. The 7th day is included.

If the customer cancels within 7 days, 10% of the total order amount will be charged.

## 5. Are highway tolls and parking fees included?

For standard airport transfer and point-to-point transfer orders, highway toll and parking fee are included by default.

Extra waiting fee, signage / placard service, child seat fee, additional stop, route change, extra hour, guide service, entrance tickets, meals, accommodation, and customer personal expenses are not included unless specifically confirmed.

## 6. What is included in a standard transfer price?

Standard transfer price normally includes:

- Driver
- Vehicle
- Fuel
- Highway toll
- Parking fee

## 7. What is not included by default?

Unless specifically confirmed, the following are not included:

- Extra waiting fee
- Signage / placard service
- Child seat fee
- Additional stop
- Route change
- Extra hour
- Guide service
- Entrance tickets
- Meals
- Accommodation
- Customer personal expenses

## 8. What is the airport pickup waiting time?

Standard airport pickup free waiting time is 90 minutes after actual flight arrival.

During peak season, free waiting time is reduced to 60 minutes after actual flight arrival.

## 9. What is the hotel or city pickup waiting time?

For airport drop-off, hotel pickup, city pickup, and point-to-point transfer, free waiting time is 30 minutes after the scheduled pickup time.

## 10. What happens if the customer is late?

If the customer exceeds the free waiting time and cannot be contacted, the order is treated as no-show and the driver may leave.

If the customer can be contacted, the driver may continue waiting only if the driver agrees. In principle, the driver has the right to leave after the free waiting time.

## 11. What is the extra waiting fee?

Extra waiting fee is JPY 3,000 per 30 minutes unless the operator manually adjusts it.

## 12. What is the night surcharge?

Night surcharge applies between 21:00 and 07:00.

Night surcharge is always +20% during night time.

Peak season is reflected through peak_price, not through an extra +50% night surcharge.

If the customer books at least 25 days before the service date, early booking protection can apply and use normal_price instead of peak_price. Night surcharge is still added if the service time is at night.

## 13. What are Tokyo and Osaka peak seasons?

Tokyo and Osaka peak seasons are:

- July
- August
- December 20 – January 5
- 5 days before and after Chinese New Year's Eve

## 14. What are Hokkaido peak seasons?

Hokkaido peak seasons are:

- December 20 – December 31
- 5 days before and after Chinese New Year's Eve

## 15. What is the signage / placard fee?

Airport signage / placard service is optional and costs JPY 2,000.

## 16. What is the child seat fee?

Child seat fee is JPY 1,000 per seat.

The AI should ask for the number of children, ages of children, and number of child seats required.

## 17. Does a baby stroller count as luggage?

Yes. A baby stroller must be counted as luggage.

## 18. How should ski equipment be handled?

Ski equipment and snowboard bags must be declared in advance.

The AI must ask for:

- Number of ski bags
- Number of snowboard bags
- Number of large suitcases
- Passenger count

If the customer has 4 passengers, 4 large suitcases, and ski/snowboard bags, HiAce is required.

## 19. Can Alphard / Vellfire carry ski equipment?

Only in limited cases, such as up to 3 passengers, up to 2 ski/snowboard bags, and limited luggage.

Otherwise, HiAce should be recommended.

## 20. Can luxury vehicles be arranged?

Luxury vehicles such as Lexus LM550, Mercedes-Benz S400, Mercedes-Benz S500, and Rolls-Royce Ghost can be arranged in Tokyo and Osaka, but final price and availability require manual operator confirmation.

Use:

```text
operator_review_required = true
quote_confidence = low
```

## 21. What are the primary customer contact channels?

Primary customer contact channels are:

- WhatsApp
- Email
- LINE

WeChat is not a primary channel for this project.

Telegram is mainly for internal operator notification.

## 22. What customer markets does JTDOS target?

JTDOS mainly targets customers from:

- Europe
- United States
- Southeast Asia

JTDOS does not primarily target Mainland China or Japan domestic markets.
