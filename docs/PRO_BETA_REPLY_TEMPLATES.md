# JTDOS Pro Beta Reply Templates

## 1. English Reply Template

Subject:

```text
Thank you for your JTDOS Pro Beta request
```

Body:

```text
Hello {{name}},

Thank you for your interest in JTDOS Pro Beta.

JTDOS Pro Beta is currently available for selected travel agencies, local operators, airport transfer companies, and Japan tourism suppliers who want to test AI booking automation with onboarding support.

To understand whether Pro Beta is a good fit, could you please share:

1. Which country and region your company is based in
2. Which Japan regions you serve or plan to serve
3. Your current monthly customer inquiry volume
4. Whether you already operate vehicles or work with local suppliers
5. Whether you need your own price table
6. Whether you need private deployment or API / JTDSS integration
7. Your current booking and operator workflow

After reviewing your use case, we can schedule a short demo call and discuss the best Pro Beta setup.

Best regards,
JTDOS Team
```

## 2. Chinese Reply Template

主题：

```text
感谢您申请 JTDOS Pro Beta
```

正文：

```text
您好 {{name}}，

感谢您申请 JTDOS Pro Beta。

目前 Pro Beta 采用人工审核和定制部署方式，主要面向旅行社、日本当地用车运营商、机场接送公司、包车公司以及有客户流量的海外代理。

为了判断 Pro Beta 是否适合您的业务，请您补充以下信息：

1. 贵公司所在国家和城市
2. 目前主要服务的日本地区
3. 每月大约收到多少客户咨询
4. 是否已有车队或合作供应商
5. 是否需要接入自己的价格表
6. 是否需要私有部署
7. 是否需要 API / JTDSS 集成
8. 目前客户咨询、报价和派单流程是如何处理的

我们会根据您的使用场景安排一次演示，并讨论适合的 Pro Beta 部署方案。

JTDOS 团队
```
