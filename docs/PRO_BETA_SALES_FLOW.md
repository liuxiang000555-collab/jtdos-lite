# JTDOS Pro Beta Sales Flow

## 1. Pro Beta Positioning

JTDOS Pro Beta is not a self-service SaaS yet.

It is a selected-access deployment package for travel agencies, local operators, airport transfer companies, and Japan tourism suppliers who want to test AI booking automation with human onboarding support.

The goal is to validate real commercial use cases before opening a fully automated signup flow.

## 2. Who It Is For

- Travel agencies
- Japan private transfer operators
- Airport pickup companies
- Charter car operators
- Overseas travel agents
- Small OTAs
- Tourism influencers with customer traffic

## 3. What Pro Beta Includes

- AI Booking Assistant
- Custom price table setup
- Multi-language customer inquiry handling
- Quote generation
- Booking request workflow
- Email / Telegram / WhatsApp notification setup
- Deployment guidance
- One onboarding session
- Optional JTDSS connector discussion

## 4. What Pro Beta Does Not Yet Include

- Fully automated self-service SaaS signup
- Automatic payment account creation
- Guaranteed Japan supplier network access
- Production JTDSS connector without private setup
- Fully automated dispatch

## 6. Sales Flow

1. Customer visits `/pricing`.
2. Customer clicks `Request Pro Beta`.
3. Customer fills the Contact form.
4. JTDOS team reviews request.
5. Schedule demo.
6. Confirm use case.
7. Confirm price.
8. Manual onboarding.
9. Deployment / setup.

## 7. Qualification Questions

- What country is your company based in?
- Do you already operate vehicles or work with suppliers?
- Which regions do you serve?
- Do you need your own price table?
- Do you need private deployment?
- Do you need API / JTDSS integration?
- How many customer inquiries do you receive per month?

## 8. Alpha Rule

Pro Beta leads are reviewed manually.

JTDOS Lite does not create paid accounts, connect real payment, or provision production integrations automatically.
