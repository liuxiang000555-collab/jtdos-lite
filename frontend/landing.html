What is JTDOS

JTDOS is a customer-facing AI sales assistant for Japan tourism transportation. It is designed for airport transfers, point-to-point transfers, one-day charter, and multi-day charter requests.

How it works

Customer describes the trip naturally.
AI identifies route, passengers, luggage, and vehicle needs.
JTDOS estimates the price and prepares a booking request.
The Japan operations team can follow up for confirmation.

Use cases

  • Airport pickup and drop-off in Tokyo, Osaka / Kansai, and Hokkaido
  • Private transfers between hotels, airports, ski resorts, and sightseeing areas
  • AI-assisted quote intake for travel agencies and local operators
