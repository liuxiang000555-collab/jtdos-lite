What is JTDOS?

JTDOS is a customer-facing AI sales assistant for Japan tourism transportation. It guides travelers and agents through airport pickup, drop-off, point-to-point transfer, one-day charter, and multi-day charter inquiries.

How it works

Natural inquiry

Customers describe routes, luggage, timing, and special needs in plain language.

Sales guidance

The assistant recommends vehicle type, asks only useful questions, and explains business rules clearly.

Booking request

When the customer is ready, JTDOS prepares a request for the Japan operations team to confirm.

Use cases

Airport transfers

Tokyo, Osaka / Kansai, and Hokkaido demo flows for common pickup and drop-off questions.

Private transfers

Hotel-to-hotel, airport-to-resort, ski destination, and sightseeing area transfer intake.

Agency sales

Show overseas partners how AI can standardize inquiry intake before operator confirmation.
